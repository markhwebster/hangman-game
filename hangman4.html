Mark's hangman game

The goal of this game is to guess a random number between 1 and 100 in less than 11 tries.

  •  +   -   Instructions, need help?
    • Press the buttons to enter your guess
    • You can also press the enter key to submit
    • We'll tell you if your guess was too high or too low. HINT: guess in the middle
    • Each time you guess wrong, you add another piece to the hangman drawing
    • At 11 guesses the poor guy gets hung
    • If you lose, press the reset button to try again with a new random number
